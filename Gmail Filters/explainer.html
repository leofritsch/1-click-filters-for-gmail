How It Works

1

In your Gmail inbox, select one or more emails by clicking the checkboxes next to them. These can be emails from different senders that you want to filter together.

2

Click "Create Filter"

When emails are selected, a red "üîç Create Filter" button appears in Gmail's toolbar. Click this button to start the automated filter creation process.

3

Watch the Magic

The extension automatically opens Gmail's advanced search, fills in all the sender email addresses, and creates a filter with recommended settings.

4

Choose a Label

The extension pre-configures the filter to skip your inbox, never go to spam, and apply to existing conversations. You just need to pick a label to keep things organized!

What Gets Configured

When you create a filter, the extension automatically sets these options:

  • From field: All email addresses from your selected emails (joined with OR)
  • Skip the Inbox: Keeps your inbox clean by archiving these emails
  • Never send to Spam: Ensures important emails don't get lost
  • Apply to existing conversations: Works retroactively on past emails
  • Apply label: You choose the label to keep things organized
